EL CID CASTILLA BEACH

Begin your escape along the soft sand beaches that frame this fabulous resort. Located in the city’s fabled Golden Zone, El Cid Castilla Beach also puts you close to authentic Mexican history and culture that reaches back to the 16th century. There’s much more to the resort than beautiful sandy beaches. Indulge in a wide range of watersports and outdoor adventures for the whole family. And what’s a vacation getaway without some me-time? To help you out, El Cid offers free babysitting as well as a fun, active Kids Club for children 4-12 and a Teens Club for ages 13-17. Each day is a chance to relax at the beach, shop our boutiques and enjoy a relaxing meal with friends. Fill your evenings with live nightly entertainment or venture into town for some local flavor. Why not start your own traditions at this sundrenched vacation paradise?

  • Eight restaurants located at El Cid properties and room service
  • El Cid’s Kids Club for ages 4-12 (From 10 am – 10 pm)  
  • El Cid’s Teens Club for ages 13-17 (From 11 am – 9 pm)
  • Live entertainment nightly
  • WiFi Internet access (Daily and weekly rates)
  • El Cid Shopping Plaza
  • Shuttle service to four El Cid Resorts & Country Club every 10 minutes
  • Free parking and valet services
  • Concierge / customer service
  • Business Center
  • Meeting facilities
  • Medical services on site
  • Laundry / dry cleaning
  • Massage and body treatments at El Cid Golf & Country Club
  • Two connecting free-form pools
  • Daily pool and beach activities
  • Aqua Sports on site (One hour of non-motorized water sports free to All-Inclusive guests
  • 24-hour currency exchange
  • ATM machine
  • Car rental
  • Horseback riding nearby
  • Easy access to facilities at neighboring El Cid El Moro and Granada hotels
  • Pronatours tour desk on site
  • Sports fishing aboard the Aries Fleet, departing from El Cid Marina daily
  • Bay cruise, Deer Island Tour and Stone Island Tours departing daily from El Cid Marina
  • Babysitting services available upon request
